How might TLC improve this workshop?3 comments

Very well organized! Thank you
The workshop felt as though it had two somewhat related goals that couldn't fully be addressed in the hour. As far as I understood it, the core insight of the workshop was that guiding students to reflect critically on AI should include a consideration of affective outcomes. This is a powerful point, but I think most participants came in expecting to tweak an assignment, and so it was hard in the workshop activities to really get a sense of the big picture. The use of multiple types of card decks may have exacerbated the confusion: I really liked learning that these resources existed, but I wonder if thinking about values versus thinking about specific assignments that engage with AI really amounts to two different workshops? But I do appreciate the TLC's programming on these issues!
